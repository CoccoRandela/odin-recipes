TRAMEZZINI SALMONE E AVOCADO

This is a quick recipe for an healthy snack or a fast lunch!

WHAT YOU NEED

  • 8 slices of toast bread
  • 400 grams of smoked salmon
  • 200 grams of cream cheese
  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 2 Beefsteak tomatoes
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

These amounts will feed 4 people

WHAT YOU DO

  1. Cut the tomato and the avocado into slices.
  2. Assemble the sandwich starting with a spoonful of cream cheese, 2-3 slices of smoked salmon, half an avocado and half a tomato.
  3. Finish up with a touch of olive oil, some salt and pepper and the sandwich is done. Told you it was easy! Enjoy!
