PASTA ALLE VONGOLE

This is a classic italian recipe that will immediately transport you to the beautiful views of the Amalfi coast!

WHAT YOU NEED

  • 500 grams of pasta, preferably linguine
  • 2 kilos of fresh clams
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • A big bunch of italian parsley
  • 200 ml of white wine
  • A quarter of a lemon
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

These amounts will feed 4 people

WHAT YOU DO

  1. Put the clams in salted water for 2 hours minumum, so that they can release the sand they have inside.
  2. In a big pot on medium-high heat, drizzle a generous amount of olive oil along with the cloves of garlic and the parsley stems.
  3. In another pot, put some water to boil and then add a handful of salt.
  4. Let the garlic brown, then quickly add the clams along with the white wine and close the lid on the pot.
  5. Keep the lid closed for 2-3 minutes, as the heat inside the pot will open the clams. If your lid is see-through you will be able to admire this gorgeous process!
  6. After most of the clams have opened, kill the heat and strain them keeping all the liquid from the pot. This is a crucial step, as this liquid will be the base for our amazing pasta sauce!
  7. In the second pot, as the water is salted and boiling, add your pasta and cook for 2 minutes less than package indications.
  8. In a shallow pan, add the garlic you retrieved from the pot along with some olive oil on low-medium heat.
  9. While the pasta and the garlic are going, finely chop some parsley and prepare your quarter lemon.
  10. When the pasta is 2 minutes from being done, strain it (keeping some of the pasta water!) and add it the pan with the garlic, raising the heat to high.
  11. Remember the liquid from the clams we saved? It's time to add it to the pasta to finish cooking it adding some of the water we kept if necessary.
  12. When the pasta is done, kill the heat, add the clams, the parsley and the juice of the lemon, saltand pepper to taste, toss the pasta in the pan like a true italian chef and Voil√†! Your pasta is ready! Enjoy!
